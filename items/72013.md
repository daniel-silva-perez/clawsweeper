## Summary

Keep #72013 open. Current main still runs startup ACP identity reconciliation on persisted pending one-shot records with stable ACP session ids, and the gateway still emits the reported warning when those checks fail. The linked PR #72023 is the right active follow-up, but it is open and not present on main, so the issue is not implemented or safely closeable yet.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #72013 open until #72023, or an equivalent maintainer patch, lands on main. The fix should skip `session.acp.mode === "oneshot"` before startup pending-identity reconciliation and keep the focused regression test proving terminal one-shot records do not increment `checked` or invoke the ACP runtime.
