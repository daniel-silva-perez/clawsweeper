## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has meaningful safeguards around browser-route errors and process-level unhandled rejections, but I did not find strong evidence that Playwright-originated unhandled rejections are fully eliminated. The remaining handler in `src/infra/unhandled-rejections.ts` still treats unknown unhandled rejections as fatal, and the issue timeline already points at a later open report for a closely related Playwright worker-target crash.

## Evidence

- **Global unhandled-rejection handler exists, but unknown rejections still exit:** `src/index.ts` installs the handler, and `src/infra/unhandled-rejections.ts` only suppresses registered handlers, `AbortError`, and transient network/SQLite cases before calling `process.exit(1)` for anything else.
  - file: [src/infra/unhandled-rejections.ts:340](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/f03252aaf9cc946dd6a4f6189f30d5c5dcd64022/src/infra/unhandled-rejections.ts#L340)
