## Summary

Current `main` still hard-blocks sandboxed sessions from spawning unsandboxed subagents, and the config/schema/docs surfaces do not expose the requested `dangerouslyAllowUnsandboxedSubagentSpawn` escape hatch. The issue is also only about 30 days old and has a linked PR reference, so none of the conservative close reasons apply.
