## Summary

Keep open. Current main still rejects persisted `auth-profiles.json` entries with `type: "aws-sdk"` as `invalid_type`; Bedrock/provider-level `auth: "aws-sdk"` support exists, but the specific #69708 store-loader marker handling is not implemented. The related PR #69753 proposed the right narrow fix but was closed unmerged as stale, and the reporter asked why it was closed.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #69708 open and land a small auth-profile store-loader fix: recognize `type: "aws-sdk"` as an SDK-managed no-secret marker, skip it without counting it as `invalid_type`, preserve warnings for genuinely invalid entries, and add the two regression tests described in #69753. Do not widen downstream credential handling unless a maintainer wants `aws-sdk` to become a first-class stored credential type.
