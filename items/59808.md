## Evidence

- **Issue is actionable and recent:** The issue includes concrete environment details, a symptom payload, reproduction steps, and a proposed code-level fix. It was opened 2026-04-02, so it is not eligible for stale_insufficient_info.
- **Current QMD mcporter path still parses raw stdout:** In the current qmd-manager mcporter path, runQmdSearchViaMcporter awaits ensureMcporterDaemonStarted(), invokes `mcporter call ... --output json`, then parses `result.stdout` with JSON.parse. I did not see a cleanup step that strips `[mcporter]` stdout log lines before parsing.
