## Summary

Keep #59245 open. Current main has outbound voice-call primitives, DTMF support, and conversation mode, but it does not implement the requested autonomous outbound task-call capability with IVR/hold orchestration, user escalation, authorized-info policy, timeout/reschedule behavior, or callback continuity. The most recent maintainer discussion also explicitly says the item remains open on current main.
