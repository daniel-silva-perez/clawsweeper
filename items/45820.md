## Summary

Current `main` strongly suggests two parts of #45820 are already addressed: Control UI auth now treats `allowInsecureAuth` as localhost-only while preserving a separate `dangerouslyDisableDeviceAuth` bypass, and the exec approval modal now has explicit viewport bounds and internal scrolling. But I could not tie the reported `main · heartbeat` giant-icon pane regression to a specific current fix with high confidence from read-only inspection alone, so this is not strong enough for a conservative close.
