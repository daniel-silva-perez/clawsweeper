## Summary

Current `main` already implements the reported audio CLI dispatch path, including shared `tools.media.models` entries gated by `capabilities`, templated CLI execution with `{{MediaPath}}`, and Feishu inbound audio download/classification fixes that would otherwise bypass audio handling.
