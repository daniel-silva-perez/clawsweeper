## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not add `precedingText` or `messageId` to the `before_tool_call` hook event. There are adjacent primitives (`llm_output.assistantTexts` plus `runId`/`toolCallId` on `before_tool_call`) that could support a plugin-side workaround, but the specific hook enhancement requested in #12617 is not implemented on `main`.
