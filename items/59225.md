Keep open. The issue is recent, concrete, and asks for a core tool-schema shaping capability rather than a stale or ClawHub-only idea. Related discussion also points to real `sessions_spawn` parameter pollution risk, and the linked PR appears to address only ACP-only field stripping rather than the requested per-agent `allowedParams`/`deniedParams` schema filtering.
