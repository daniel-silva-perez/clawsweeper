## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the requested Telegram unknown-group warn/leave feature, and the proposed PR still appears to have unresolved correctness gaps against current Telegram access/threading behavior.

## Evidence

- **Feature absent on main:** A repo search for `unknownGroupAction`, `unknownGroupMessage`, `unknownGroupCooldown`, `UnknownGroup`, and `unknown group` returned no matches in `extensions/telegram/src`, `src/config/types.telegram.ts`, `src/config/zod-schema.providers-core.ts`, or `docs`, so the requested config/runtime surface is not implemented on current `main`.
