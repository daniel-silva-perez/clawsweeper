## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hard-blocks sandboxed requesters from spawning unsandboxed subagents, and the proposed `dangerouslyAllowUnsandboxedSubagentSpawn` config does not exist in code, schema, docs, or tests.

## Evidence

- **Current spawn logic still forbids the requested behavior:** `spawnSubagentDirect` rejects any unsandboxed child when the requester is sandboxed, with no escape-hatch flag check. It separately rejects `sandbox="require"` for unsandboxed targets.
  - file: [src/agents/subagent-spawn.ts:695](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/8f64cd3e4d8339a6aab3ca230d5e14944c91cfaf/src/agents/subagent-spawn.ts#L695)

- **Sandbox runtime/config types do not expose the proposed flag:** Resolved sandbox config returns only mode/backend/access/visibility/scope/docker/ssh/browser/prune, and `AgentSandboxConfig` likewise has no `dangerouslyAllowUnsandboxedSubagentSpawn` field.
  - file: [src/agents/sandbox/config.ts:255](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/8f64cd3e4d8339a6aab3ca230d5e14944c91cfaf/src/agents/sandbox/config.ts#L255)
