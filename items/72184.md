## Summary

Keep #72184 open. Current main still writes queued outbound ReplyPayloads before delivery and recovery replays `entry.payloads` directly with `skipQueue: true`; I did not find a current-main guard that drops or sanitizes a recovered payload containing raw session/bootstrap/model-input context. A prior local ClawSweeper review for the same item also kept it open, and the diff from that reviewed SHA to current HEAD does not touch the relevant outbound recovery files.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and fix it in the outbound delivery/recovery path. The conservative product path is to preserve only safe, sendable final reply payloads for recovery, fail closed or move to failed when recovered text looks like raw trace/session/bootstrap/model-input context, and add a regression test that simulates a queued Telegram delivery containing raw context after a restart. If raw trace enqueueing is the root cause, keep raw trace payloads out of recovery replay unless explicitly intended and safe.
