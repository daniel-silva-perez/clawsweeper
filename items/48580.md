## Summary

Current `main` has ACPX runtime hardening and a much newer bundled `acpx` version, but I did not find strong repo-local evidence that the specific direct CLI failure in #48580 (`acpx codex sessions new` exiting immediately because Codex needs a TTY, while session metadata stays open) is fixed well enough to close conservatively.
