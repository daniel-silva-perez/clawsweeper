## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the pre-PR `/new` behavior: TUI `/new` generates a fresh `tui-<uuid>` key and only calls `setSession(...)`, while hook emission still lives inside `performGatewaySessionReset(...)`. The PR’s added `hookSourceKey` plumbing and updated tests are not present on `main`, and there is no local changelog/history evidence that this landed another way.

## Evidence

The `reset` branch separately calls `await client.resetSession(state.currentSessionKey, name);`.
