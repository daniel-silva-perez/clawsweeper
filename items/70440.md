## Summary

Keep #70440 open. Current main has targeted incomplete-turn mitigations, but it still does not provide the requested global or per-channel policy to silently swallow genuine empty-turn fallback errors, and the maintainer comment explicitly says the feature remains valid after the #70396/#70623 fix.

## Best Possible Solution

Add an explicit empty-turn/incomplete-turn delivery policy at the runner or outbound delivery boundary, with schema/docs/tests for global and per-surface or per-channel overrides. Keep `error` as the default, allow `silent` for voice/TTS-heavy surfaces such as Signal, preserve server-side WARN logs and terminal metadata, and avoid conflating this with the existing `NO_REPLY` silent-reply policy or Telegram-only delivery error controls.
