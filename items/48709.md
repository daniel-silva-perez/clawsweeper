## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` appears to mitigate the `<think>` suppression and mixed text+tool delivery parts, but the Gemini `textSignature` bloat report is not convincingly fixed: the Google transport still stores Gemini thought signatures on assistant text blocks and replays them back on same-model history, while the replay sanitizers only target `thoughtSignature`/`thinkingSignature`, not `textSignature`.

## Evidence
