## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has partial upgrade safety tooling, but it does not implement the requested native pre/post-update compatibility scanner with risk scoring, machine-readable reporting, and smoke recommendations.

## Evidence

- **Doctor CLI has no upgrade-scan/report flags:** `doctor` currently exposes `--yes`, `--repair`/`--fix`, `--force`, `--non-interactive`, `--generate-gateway-token`, and `--deep`. There is no `--upgrade-scan`, `--json`, risk-report, or smoke-recommendation option.
  - file: [src/cli/program/register.maintenance.ts:13](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/a59d1bd46d9fc0bd2d9194b3f17db2561efc51e3/src/cli/program/register.maintenance.ts#L13)

They do not document a pre-update scan, post-update risk score, machine-readable upgrade report, or rollback recommendation workflow.
