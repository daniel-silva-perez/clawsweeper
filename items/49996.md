## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the reported strict-mode tail-drop behavior for unclosed reasoning tags, and the Telegram delivery path can still suppress the whole reply when reasoning is hidden and no answer text survives stripping. The submitted PR is also far too broad for conservative maintainer cleanup.
