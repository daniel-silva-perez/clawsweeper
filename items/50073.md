## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has multi-agent and per-agent skill primitives, but it does not implement the issue's requested coordinator capability index or direct skill-based routing. The issue is also still active: a maintainer comment on April 12, 2026 linked ongoing related orchestration work instead of indicating the request was obsolete or out of scope.
