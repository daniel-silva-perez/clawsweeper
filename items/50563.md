## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still marks WhatsApp inbound messages as read before OpenClaw finishes processing them, and the retry/dedupe guard is memory-only for this path. A crash between `readMessages()` and the later `onMessage`/dedupe commit would still leave no durable record to replay on restart.

## Risks / Open Questions

- Closing this now would hide a still-plausible data-loss window on WhatsApp inbound processing after `readMessages()` but before durable persistence or successful handler completion.
- There is no evidence on current `main` of a shipped WAL, persist-before-ack queue, or equivalent crash-recovery mechanism for this inbound path.
