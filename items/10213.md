## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has server-side compaction support documented and wired for OpenAI Responses, but not for Anthropic. The Anthropic plugin only exposes beta-header, fast-mode, and service-tier wrappers, and the Anthropic transport does not handle any compaction-specific request/response shape.

## Evidence
