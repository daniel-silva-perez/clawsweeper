## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` shows explicit support for aborting active runs and queuing follow-up messages in WebChat/Control UI, and the docs describe DM continuity via `session.dmScope`/`session.identityLinks`. But this issue bundles three concerns together, and the QQ portion names NapCat/OneBot, which does not exist in this repo checkout. That leaves insufficient evidence to say the whole item is fixed or obsolete with conservative maintainer confidence.
