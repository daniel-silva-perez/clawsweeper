## Summary

## Best Possible Solution

Close #54640 as superseded by #54647 and keep review/implementation on that PR. The best code direction is to make `buildGatewaySessionRow()` or its model identity helper ACP-aware by reading `entry.acp.runtimeOptions.model` first and suppressing the default-model fallback for ACP sessions with unknown runtime model.
