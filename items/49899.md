## Summary

Keep open. Current main mitigates the underlying oversized-tool-result risk by capping persisted live tool results and documenting `agents.defaults.contextLimits.toolResultMaxChars`, but `openclaw doctor` still does not implement the requested diagnostic that scans session transcripts for oversized persisted `toolResult` buildup, shared-lane concentration, or degradation signals.
