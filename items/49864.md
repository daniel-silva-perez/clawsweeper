## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has conflicting Telegram `requireMention` logic: the inbound runtime in `extensions/telegram/src/bot-message-context.ts` applies `activationOverride` before `topicConfig?.requireMention`, while the shared Telegram group-policy helper, docs, and tests all say topic overrides should win. That is not strong evidence for closure; it is evidence the report is still plausible on `main`.

## Evidence
