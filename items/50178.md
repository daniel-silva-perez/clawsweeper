## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not provide strong evidence that Qianfan's `reasoning_content` + tool-call replay bug is fixed. Qianfan still uses the generic OpenAI-compatible completions path, there is no Qianfan-specific runtime/replay hook in the bundled plugin, and the regression coverage in-tree targets OpenAI Responses reasoning replay and Google `thought_signature`, not Qianfan completions replay.
