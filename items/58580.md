## Summary

Keep open. Current main has related diagnostics, session-store maintenance, and restart-interruption recovery, but I did not find the requested behavior: detecting a channel/session that keeps receiving messages without successful replies for N minutes and automatically clearing or pruning only that stuck session. The issue is recent, concrete, author association is NONE, and the lone comment corroborates the incident pattern while adding actionable design notes around soft reset, slow-vs-stuck detection, alerting, and configurability.
