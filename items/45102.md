## Summary

Keep open. I did not find strong evidence that current `main` fixes this report. The gateway still has synchronous full-transcript read paths on the hot path, which keeps the reported slowdown/timeouts plausible for large or repeated voice/STT workloads, and the adjacent release notes are diagnostics/mitigation work rather than a clear fix for this issue.

## Evidence
