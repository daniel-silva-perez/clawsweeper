## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still rotates profiles or falls back immediately on 429/rate-limit failures; it does not implement the PR's same-profile exponential-backoff retry behavior or the proposed `extractRetryAfterMs()` helper.

There is no same-profile sleep/backoff retry before rotation.
