## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still treats timeout failures as auth-profile/model-fallback events and ultimately errors once the fallback chain is exhausted; the only explicit same-model retry I found is the narrow idle-timeout path, not a general retry with backoff after MiniMax timeout exhaustion.

## Evidence
