## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` partially addressed the originally reported Telegram/internal-hook hole, but the broader inconsistency in outbound sent-hook coverage is still present. Telegram preview-finalized replies still emit only the internal `message:sent` hook, and the shared plugin-handled outbound path still bypasses both centralized `message:sent` and `message_sent` emission.

## Evidence

- **Partial Telegram fix shipped:** The changelog records a Telegram-only internal-hook bridge: "Telegram/direct delivery: bridge direct delivery sends to internal `message:sent` hooks...". That is a narrow internal-hook fix, not a full resolution of all outbound paths or plugin `message_sent` coverage.
