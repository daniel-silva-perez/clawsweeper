## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has newer chat empty-state rendering and tighter chat/mobile layout code, but this read-only review did not find a bug-specific fix or a direct non-repro path for the reported blank chat area with black triangle artifact. The evidence is adjacent and suggestive, not strong enough for a conservative close.
