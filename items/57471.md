## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still handles `LiveSessionModelSwitchError` by recording a `candidate_failed` attempt and continuing the existing fallback chain instead of jumping directly to the live-session target model.

## Evidence

- **Fallback loop still treats live switch as failed candidate:** `src/agents/model-fallback.ts:921`-`948` catches `LiveSessionModelSwitchError`, wraps it as `FailoverError`, calls `recordFailedCandidateAttempt(...)`, and `continue`s to the next candidate. There is no redirect or candidate reordering in that branch.
  - file: [src/agents/model-fallback.ts:921](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/5dfc1b90e176a9e4d13b9e91ad6affec6b92f213/src/agents/model-fallback.ts#L921)

  - sha: [5dfc1b90e176](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/5dfc1b90e176a9e4d13b9e91ad6affec6b92f213)

  - file: [src/agents/model-fallback-observation.ts:30](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/5dfc1b90e176a9e4d13b9e91ad6affec6b92f213/src/agents/model-fallback-observation.ts#L30)
