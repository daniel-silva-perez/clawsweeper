## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still lacks YAML frontmatter in `skills/canvas/SKILL.md`, and the bundled-skill loader drops skills that do not provide a parsed `description`. The PR’s change still addresses a real gap on `main` rather than something already implemented or obsolete.
