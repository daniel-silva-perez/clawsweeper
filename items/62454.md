## Summary

Current `main` does not contain this PR, and the proposed changes conflict with existing tested behavior in two areas while the cron recovery heuristic appears unsafe for legitimate long-running runs. This is not strong-close territory; the PR should stay open for follow-up or revision rather than maintainer cleanup closure.
