## Summary

Current `main` fixes the specific out-of-order entries in `docs/channels/index.md` and `docs/providers/index.md`, but `docs/docs.json` still contains non-alphabetical channel page lists. Because the issue is about alphabetical ordering violations in channel and provider lists, the evidence is not strong enough to close it as fully implemented.
