## Summary

Current `main` has partial related capability: node-level `screen.snapshot` and `screen.record` exist, with macOS snapshot capture implemented and node screen recording documented. But I did not find evidence of continuous real-time screen sharing or a documented assistant-facing flow equivalent to the issue’s requested live screen sharing. Conservative cleanup bar for closing is not met.
