## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a completed fix for long-lived proxy/idleness failures on the webchat streaming path. The OpenAI-compatible webchat streams still open SSE and then wait for assistant deltas without periodic keepalive writes, while another Gateway SSE surface (`sessions-history`) already uses `: keepalive` heartbeats every 15s. That matches the report's proxy-idle failure shape and there is not enough evidence that #49919 is implemented or obsolete on `main`.
