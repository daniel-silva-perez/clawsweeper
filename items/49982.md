## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still rejects oversized image attachments and does not auto-compress them before sending or storing.

## Evidence

- **Gateway still hard-rejects attachments above 5,000,000 bytes:** `parseMessageWithAttachments()` estimates decoded size and throws `attachment <label>: exceeds size limit (...)` before any resize/compression path.
  - file: [src/gateway/chat-attachments.ts:338](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/45146913007da723b4f06aba6a66cf4a695974c0/src/gateway/chat-attachments.ts#L338)
  - command: `nl -ba src/gateway/chat-attachments.ts | sed -n '320,420p'`
  - sha: [45146913007d](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/45146913007da723b4f06aba6a66cf4a695974c0)
- **Media store still enforces a 5MB ceiling:** `MEDIA_MAX_BYTES` is `5 * 1024 * 1024`, and `saveMediaBuffer()` throws `Media exceeds 5MB limit` when the buffer is larger.
  - file: [src/media/store.ts:16](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/45146913007da723b4f06aba6a66cf4a695974c0/src/media/store.ts#L16)
