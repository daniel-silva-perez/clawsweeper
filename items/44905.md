## Summary

Keep open. Current main fixes the commentary-phase subset and the older NO_REPLY-with-media gap, but it still does not have a universal or Discord-specific outbound sanitizer for raw tool-envelope/control-token text, so the broader Discord leak family in #44905 is not strongly resolved.

## Evidence
