## Summary

Keep open. Current main still matches the reported broader WhatsApp regression: the narrow quiet-session watchdog fix for #70678 is present, but active-listener gaps, fast outbound failures during reconnect, health-monitor restarts of disconnected/reconnecting state, one-shot group hydration failure, and Bad MAC/session recovery gaps remain. The discussion is recent and cross-platform, and the related open maintainer PR explicitly says related reports including this issue should remain open unless their exact symptoms are proven fixed.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open as a real WhatsApp plugin regression. Preserve the #70678 transport-activity watchdog mitigation, then add focused fixes and tests for retryable 408/428 reconnect churn, health-monitor coordination while WhatsApp is already reconnecting, outbound delivery behavior during short listener gaps, and recovery from `groupFetchAllParticipating` 1006 plus Baileys/libsignal Bad MAC failures so group events and sends do not silently disappear. Review #72621 as a narrow related candidate, but do not treat it as closing this broader report unless follow-up evidence covers these exact symptoms.
