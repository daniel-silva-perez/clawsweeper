## Summary

Keep open. Current main includes related Telegram polling and channel-health improvements, and the `channels.<id>.healthMonitor.enabled` config now exists, but the evidence is not strong enough to say #50174 is fixed end-to-end. In particular, Discord still treats `connected === false` as a restart-worthy `disconnected` state, so the reported endless Discord restart loop remains plausible, and I could not verify a Windows-native reproduction path in this read-only environment.
