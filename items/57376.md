## Summary

Current `main` still appears to expose non-session store entries through `sessions.list`, so the report should stay open.

## Evidence

- **sessions.list still enumerates every store entry:** `listSessionsFromStore` starts from `Object.entries(store)` and only filters cron/global/unknown, agent, spawnedBy, and label. There is no guard requiring `sessionId` or non-null `updatedAt` before returning rows.
  - file: [src/gateway/session-utils.ts:1483](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/5dfc1b90e176a9e4d13b9e91ad6affec6b92f213/src/gateway/session-utils.ts#L1483)

  - sha: [5dfc1b90e176](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/5dfc1b90e176a9e4d13b9e91ad6affec6b92f213)

  - file: [src/gateway/session-utils.ts:1247](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/5dfc1b90e176a9e4d13b9e91ad6affec6b92f213/src/gateway/session-utils.ts#L1247)
