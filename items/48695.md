## Summary

Keep open. Current main documents supported headless-VPS browser setups, compact snapshots, and explicit wait primitives, but that does not prove the reported AVA ERP modal/state problem is fixed or not reproducible. The report also mixes multiple different concerns: OpenClaw-managed browser setup, a custom Playwright script against a private third-party app, token-cost tuning, and a DigitalOcean Marketplace image user/config mismatch. That is not strong enough evidence for `implemented_on_main`, `cannot_reproduce`, `clawhub`, `incoherent`, or `stale_insufficient_info` under the conservative close policy.
