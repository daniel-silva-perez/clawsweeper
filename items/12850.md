## Summary

Keep open. On `origin/main` (the pinned `33615934` main), Mattermost still builds the inbound system-event preview with `bodyText.replace(/\s+/g, " ").slice(0, 160)`, which flattens newlines, and there is no Mattermost config/schema surface for an optional newline-preserving preview mode.

  - file: [extensions/mattermost/src/mattermost/monitor.ts:1478](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/3361593442e295e9bb1f55cbfb38861ea9413e45/extensions/mattermost/src/mattermost/monitor.ts#L1478)
