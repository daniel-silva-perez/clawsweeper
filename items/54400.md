## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does have MiniMax image-understanding plumbing, but it is still centered on the plugin-owned `MiniMax-VL-01` path, not the issue's requested direct `imageModel: minimax/MiniMax-M2.5` flow. The strongest evidence in-tree still shows the bundled MiniMax chat catalog as text-only, `isMinimaxVlmModel()` only special-casing `MiniMax-VL-01`, and onboarding only materializing image-capable M2.7 variants. That is not strong enough to conclude #54400 is implemented on `main`.
