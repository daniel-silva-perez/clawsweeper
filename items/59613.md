## Summary

Keep open. The issue is recent, concrete, externally corroborated in the discussion, and current main still appears to mount the sandbox workspace read-only when `workspaceAccess` is `none`, matching the reported Docker bind `/workspace:ro,z` behavior rather than disproving it.
