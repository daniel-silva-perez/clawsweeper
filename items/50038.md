## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` appears to have addressed several concrete regressions called out in the issue: `allowAgents` is now in the config schema and docs, cross-agent subagent workspace selection is covered, and subagent announce routing/delivery has explicit fallback and nested-orchestrator coverage. But I could not find evidence of the remaining "delegate-only" boundary request (`tools.selfDeny` or equivalent). The current docs/code still describe per-agent tool policy and subagent-depth session-tool gating, not a direct parent-can-spawn-workers-with-`exec` while the parent itself cannot use `exec` model. Because the issue is an umbrella over multiple concerns and at least that feature gap still looks real, the evidence is not strong enough to close.
