## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still immediately classifies a running disconnected channel as unhealthy after startup grace and restarts it; the reconnect-grace behavior from PR #45712 is not implemented on `f03252aaf9cc946dd6a4f6189f30d5c5dcd64022`.
