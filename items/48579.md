## Summary

Keep open. Current main makes two things clear: `contextPruning.mode: "off"` is respected, and pruning is a separate system from compaction. But the issue's core symptom is not just that misunderstanding; it is repeated compaction at roughly 12-16% reported usage on Anthropic API-key sessions. I found related post-report fixes for compaction-setting retention and context-window/threshold handling, but not strong enough evidence that #48579 is fully fixed on `main` or safe to close conservatively.

## Risks / Open Questions

- Closing now would conflate a likely misunderstanding about pruning with an unresolved runtime symptom: repeated compactions far below the expected 1M-token budget.
- There are related fixes in current main for compaction override retention and context-window handling, which suggests the report may be partially addressed, but I do not have enough evidence to map those fixes directly to this Anthropic API-key scenario.
- No current-main reproduction was performed in this read-only review, so I cannot confidently say the aggressive-compaction symptom no longer occurs.
