## Summary

Keep open. Current main partially covers the report: Telegram DM ACP session-mode spawns can bind directly to the DM conversation. But the specific handoff semantics in the issue are not fully evidenced on main: `sessions_spawn` still has no `bind` parameter, parent-owned ACP children are still documented as background runs, and bound ACP conversations reset the same ACP session in place rather than auto-reverting to the parent session.

## Evidence

- **Telegram DM ACP spawns now bind to the DM conversation:** `spawnAcpDirect({ runtime: "acp", mode: "session", thread: true })` is tested to bind a Telegram direct-message conversation with `placement: "current"` and DM conversation id `6098642967`. This shows the old DM restriction is at least partially gone.
