## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not include the PR's invalid-thinking-signature replay fix or the retry fallback. The replay sanitizer still goes straight from image sanitization to optional `dropThinkingBlocks`, Bedrock Claude replay still preserves signatures, and the focused tests for stripping invalid signatures are absent.
