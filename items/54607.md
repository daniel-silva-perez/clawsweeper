## Summary

Keep open. Current main appears to have fixed the underlying auto-enable behavior and setup UX around disabled plugin-backed channels, but the documentation request in #54607 is still not implemented clearly enough. BlueBubbles docs explain that the channel is plugin-backed and configured under `channels.bluebubbles`, yet they do not document `plugins.entries.bluebubbles.enabled`, do not explain the two disablement layers in one place, and do not provide the requested safe local loopback-only example.
