## Summary

Do not close. Current `main` contains partial mitigations for stray `HEARTBEAT_OK` output and for heartbeat prompt injection on embedded runs, but the codebase still has an ungated CLI/runtime path that injects heartbeat guidance on ordinary runs. That means the reported symptom is not strongly proven fixed on `main` yet.

## Evidence
