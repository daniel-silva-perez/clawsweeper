## Summary

Keep open. Current main does not implement the server-rendered outbound status footer or context-threshold warnings from #72005, and the PR is a plausible core outbound-pipeline change for the #71984 problem. It needs revisions for the review findings rather than cleanup closure.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #72005 open for author or maintainer revision. The current-main product gap remains real.
