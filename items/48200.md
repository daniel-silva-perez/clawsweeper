## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the PR's `accountHandle` safeguard, and the existing iMessage loop protections still rely on either `is_from_me` being true or a previously seen self-chat copy. That means the reported personal-account/multi-device `is_from_me:false` self-echo case remains plausible and unproven on `main`.
