## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does suppress commentary-phase leakage in several runtime/history paths, but it does not implement the broader outbound pre-send policy requested in #50690: there is no execution-state-backed ack gate, repeated-ack suppression, progress-debt timeout, or typed outbound policy layer in the delivery surface I inspected.

## Evidence

- **Shared phase-aware text extraction now drops commentary-only output:** `src/shared/chat-message-content.ts` introduces explicit `commentary` vs `final_answer` phases and `extractAssistantVisibleText`, which prefers final-answer text and returns nothing for commentary-only assistant text.
  - file: [src/shared/chat-message-content.ts:22](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/81666e586aa6d4305e6472d7075e62f63f701831/src/shared/chat-message-content.ts#L22)

  - file: [src/gateway/server-methods/chat.ts:1213](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/81666e586aa6d4305e6472d7075e62f63f701831/src/gateway/server-methods/chat.ts#L1213)
