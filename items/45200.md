## Summary

Keep open. Current main still drops subagent completion results after announce give-up without a user-facing fallback. The live and resumed give-up paths only mark delivery failed, log, and finish cleanup; there is no replacement notify path on `main`, and subagent tasks are explicitly excluded from the generic terminal auto-delivery system.

## Evidence
