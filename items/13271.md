## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has channel/group-scoped tool policy and explicit subagent orchestration, but I found no per-channel per-turn tool-call budget or automatic overflow delegation. The request is coherent, core-scoped, and not implemented well enough to close as fixed or obsolete.

## Evidence
