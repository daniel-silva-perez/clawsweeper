## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still routes every `/v1/chat/completions` call through the normal persisted agent/session pipeline: it generates a session key even for anonymous requests, resolves/persists session store entries and transcript files, and persists `skillsSnapshot` for new sessions. I did not find any `chatCompletions` passthrough or ephemeral-mode config on `main`.
