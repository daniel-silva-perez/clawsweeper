## Summary

Current `main` does not give strong evidence to close this. The original reliable repro in the issue comments shifted away from TUI and toward separate `gateway probe` / `status --all` flows, and those CLI paths are now explicitly read-scoped on `main`. But the TUI gateway client still does not explicitly request `operator.read`, so I cannot say the TUI-side report is implemented/fixed or obsolete with high confidence.
