## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still contains the reported failure path: remote/snapshot routes gate on gateway-side `requirePwAi`, and Playwright loading still soft-caches `null` with no retry. I found no evidence on `main` or since `v2026.4.22` that this issue was implemented or made obsolete.
