## Summary

Current `main` does not generically turn assistant-side business rejections into model-fallback failures. Unclassified `stopReason: "error"` assistant results still return normally from `runEmbeddedPiAgent`, so `runWithModelFallback` can still mark the candidate as succeeded and stop the chain. The recent coverage on `main` only proves fallback for specific classified assistant errors like OpenRouter `No endpoints found`, not for the reported CE-011-style business rejection.
