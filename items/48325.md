## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not include the proposed `gateway-probe` bundled plugin or its Kafka dependency, and the closest shipped feature is the separate `diagnostics-otel` bundled exporter. The repo’s current docs also do not make the needed diagnostics/log transport seam a clearly supported external-plugin path, so `clawhub` is not strong enough for a conservative close.
