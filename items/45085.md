## Close Comment

Closing this as implemented on `main` after Codex review. The reported `openclaw status` warmup path is no longer active: `src/agents/context.ts:133` includes `status` in `SKIP_EAGER_WARMUP_PRIMARY_COMMANDS`, and `src/agents/context.lookup.test.ts:217` explicitly asserts that `openclaw status --json` does not eager-warm the context-window cache.
