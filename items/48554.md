## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still routes proxied audio transcription through `resolveProxyFetchFromEnv()`, and that wrapper passes `RequestInit.body` straight into `undici.fetch` without the FormData normalization now used elsewhere. The newer normalization in `src/infra/net/runtime-fetch.ts` appears to cover guarded dispatcher paths such as batch uploads, but it is not wired into the audio proxy-fetch path described in the issue.
