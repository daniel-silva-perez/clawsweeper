## Summary

Keep open. This is an open ClawHub issue, and the repository-specific cleanup policy only permits auto-closing pull requests that are certainly implemented on main; all ClawHub issues should remain open for maintainer handling. The current source does contain owner/staff mechanisms for the underlying request: skills can be soft-deleted through the v1 API/CLI, owners can merge duplicate owned listings, and staff can hide or hard-delete skills. But the request is for production removal of three named skills, so the source tree cannot prove the production records were actually removed. The related open ValueScan issue #1758 also covers duplicate skill removal plus search/moderation concerns, so this should stay in the maintainer support queue rather than be auto-closed.

Have a maintainer or ClawHub staff member handle this as production support: verify ValueScan-io owns the three listed slugs, use the owner delete API/CLI or staff management tooling to soft-delete or merge the old listings, coordinate with the related #1758 moderation/search appeal, and close manually once production reflects the requested cleanup.
