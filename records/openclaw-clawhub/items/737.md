## Clownfish Work Prompt

Fix public stale duplicate/canonical relation leakage for ClawHub skills. The observable bug is that `@nexecuteinc/cmdbcompass` can show `duplicate of @nexecuteinc/cmdb-compass` even though the old slug is not public. In `convex/skills.ts`, update the public `getBySlug` relation hydration so `forkOf` and `canonical` are returned only when the related skill passes the same public visibility rules used by `toPublicSkill`/`isPublicSkillDoc` and its owner is public. Do not mutate production data, do not remove the duplicate schema fields, and do not change hard-delete semantics. Mention in the PR that staff still needs to clear the existing production row for this specific skill if they want the persisted association removed.
