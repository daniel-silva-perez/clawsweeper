## What This Changes

## Best Possible Solution

Maintain the conservative scanner behavior until maintainers decide otherwise, then add clear publisher-facing guidance explaining why VirusTotal and ClawScan can disagree, how to request rescans or false-positive review, and what skill patterns reduce flags; use staff clean overrides for confirmed false positives and only change moderation logic with narrow tests for a specific safe scanner-signal class.
