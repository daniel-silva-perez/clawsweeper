## What This Changes

The issue asks why the reporter’s `agent-memory-sr` skill remains marked suspicious even though VirusTotal and ClawHub’s scanner reported benign/high-confidence results.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open for maintainer/security review of the specific `agent-memory-sr` finding.
