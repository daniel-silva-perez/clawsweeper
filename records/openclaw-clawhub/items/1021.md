## Summary

Keep open. ClawHub cleanup policy keeps issues open in this lane, and current main still leaves the ClawScan/OpenClaw `Error` status under-explained while the moderation behavior for scanner-side failures remains a security/product decision.

## What This Changes

The issue asks ClawHub to explain the OpenClaw/ClawScan `Error` scan status, provide author remediation or rescan guidance, and avoid treating scanner-side failures as suspicious skill content without clear policy.

## Best Possible Solution

Define a safe ClawScan/OpenClaw `Error` contract, surface sanitized explanation and next steps on the skill page and scanner detail page, document web/API/CLI rescan and appeal paths, and explicitly decide how scanner-side failures interact with static suspicious findings in coordination with #669.
