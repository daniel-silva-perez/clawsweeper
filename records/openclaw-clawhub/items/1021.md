## Summary

Keep open. ClawHub cleanup policy does not auto-close issues in this lane, and current main still leaves the author-facing ClawScan/OpenClaw `Error` state under-explained while scanner-failure moderation behavior remains a maintainer/security policy decision.

## What This Changes

The issue asks ClawHub to explain the OpenClaw/ClawScan `Error` scan status, provide author remediation or rescan guidance, and decide whether scanner-side failures should flag skills as suspicious.

## Best Possible Solution

Define a safe ClawScan/OpenClaw `Error` contract, surface sanitized explanation and next steps on the skill page and scanner detail page, document web/API/CLI rescan and appeal paths, and explicitly decide how scanner-side failures interact with static suspicious findings in coordination with #669.
