Keep open. This is an open ClawHub issue, and the repository-specific cleanup policy only permits auto-closing pull requests that are certainly implemented on current main. The source now has generic VT polling, stale-scan fallback, owner-facing pending-scan errors, owner rescan requests, and soft-delete-aware scan candidate selection, but those do not prove the production `clawdraw` record was recovered.
