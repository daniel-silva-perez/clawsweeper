## Summary

Keep open. The linked Apple Notes implementation is already available as the published ClawHub skill `apple-notes-native`, and current ClawHub registry/install/metadata surfaces appear capable of hosting a macOS osascript-based skill. The unresolved request, however, is maintainer product judgment about replacing or recommending an integrated memo-based Apple Notes path, and the repository policy for `openclaw/clawhub` says to keep issues open rather than auto-close them.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open for maintainer product review. The published `apple-notes-native` skill can remain the community-distributed ClawHub path, while maintainers decide whether to document it as a recommended alternative or track an OpenClaw core change to replace the memo-based Apple Notes default after evaluating reliability, setup friction, and macOS automation security.
