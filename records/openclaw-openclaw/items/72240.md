## Summary

Keep open. Current main has internal timeout/signal plumbing and an `exec.process.completed` diagnostic event, but foreground exec failures still surface raw SIGKILL as a generic signal failure and structured tool details omit `exitSignal`, `failureKind`, termination reason, timeout limit, and log/run pointers. The related open reports #66399 and #69242 overlap, but they do not fully supersede this macOS user-facing diagnostics request.

Keep this issue open and address it with a focused exec diagnostics improvement: expose structured signal/termination metadata in foreground and background exec results, preserve useful output tail, and add a log/run correlation point. Leave graceful SIGTERM escalation and broad process-supervisor semantics to #66399 unless maintainers explicitly combine the work.
