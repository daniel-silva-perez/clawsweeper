Keep open. Current main still has the generic compat-based keyword stripping path, but the Volcengine Ark/Kimi catalog rows do not declare the compat metadata needed to strip `minLength` before tool schemas are sent to those OpenAI-compatible endpoints; the direct fix PRs are closed unmerged, and the remaining open Kimi PR tracks a different schema-compaction problem.
