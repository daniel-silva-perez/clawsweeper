## Summary

Keep open. Current main still documents Mattermost channel/group/DM support and has the nominal WebSocket `posted` parser plus channel/group dispatch path, but I did not find source, test, release, or related-PR proof that the reported `chatmode: "oncall"` plus `groupPolicy: "open"` public/group @mention regression is fixed. #72282 was closed back to this canonical regression, while the open keepalive/liveness work is adjacent and does not specifically prove channel @mention delivery.

## What This Changes

Mattermost should receive and process public/group channel WebSocket messages, especially @mentions in `oncall` mode with open group policy, instead of silently dropping them while DMs work.

## Best Possible Solution

Mattermost should reliably process allowed public, private, and group `posted` WebSocket frames, including @mentioned `oncall` posts with open group policy, with focused regression coverage and redacted diagnostics when a frame is filtered or never reaches the parser.
