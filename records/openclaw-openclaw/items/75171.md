Current main exports channel reply helpers from `openclaw/plugin-sdk/channel-reply-pipeline`, but legacy external plugins importing from `openclaw/plugin-sdk` or `openclaw/plugin-sdk/compat` still route through `src/plugin-sdk/root-alias.cjs` and `src/plugin-sdk/compat.ts`, where `createReplyPrefixContext` and related channel reply helpers are missing during the active migration window. Do not change bundled Feishu behavior as the primary fix, do not remove the deprecation warning, and do not broaden unrelated SDK surfaces.
