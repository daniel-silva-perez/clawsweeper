The PR updates `parseGoogleChatInboundPayload` in `extensions/googlechat/src/monitor-webhook.ts` to recognize `chat.addedToSpacePayload` and `chat.removedFromSpacePayload` alongside `chat.messagePayload` and map them to Google Chat lifecycle event types.
