## Risks / Open Questions

- The proposed `preserveFunnel` behavior intentionally preserves an externally managed public Funnel route in serve mode, so maintainers should confirm the opt-in exposure policy is acceptable for token-auth users.
- The parser depends on Tailscale `funnel status --json` output shape, especially `AllowFunnel` and `Web.*.Handlers.*.Proxy`; unit coverage helps, but upstream/live validation would reduce residual risk.
- When a Funnel route is preserved, OpenClaw skips applying a Serve route and therefore skips `resetOnExit` cleanup for that run; the PR documents/logs this, but the operator-facing semantics still need maintainer review.
