## Best Possible Solution

Capture Matrix startup logs, `openclaw channels status --probe`, `openclaw matrix verify status --verbose`, room encryption state, and whether inbound events reach `room.event`, `room.message`, `room.decrypted_event`, or `room.failed_decryption`.
