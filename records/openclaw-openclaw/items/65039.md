## Summary

Keep open. Current main still matches the reported pre-Ready failure surface: Discord voice joins pass `daveEncryption=false` straight through to `@discordjs/voice`, wait a fixed 15 seconds for `Ready`, destroy the connection on the `entersState` abort, and only run DAVE receive recovery after a connection is already Ready. The linked discussion also distinguishes this from the older Carbon event-forwarding gap and the merged post-Ready receive/reply recovery work.

## What This Changes

The issue reports that Discord `/vc join` aborts before the voice session reaches `Ready` when the voice WebSocket closes after `Hello`, especially on the DAVE-disabled path.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as an active Discord voice join bug and make the join policy explicit in the Discord plugin: either make DAVE-disabled joins reliably reach `Ready`, or fail/warn with a clear diagnostic when that mode is unsupported, with any timeout/retry behavior reflected in config, docs, and focused voice manager tests. Treat this separately from post-Ready DAVE receive recovery and from the later TTS provider issue.
