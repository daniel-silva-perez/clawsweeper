## Summary

Current main still uses the reported Baileys RC9 WhatsApp QR login path without the proposed sendUnifiedSession/auth-handshake patch, and #74349 is an open unmerged closing PR for this exact failure. ## What This Changes

The issue requests a fix for WhatsApp QR pairing that stalls after QR scan on OpenClaw’s Baileys-based WhatsApp plugin, leaving only the “WhatsApp QR received.” log and no connected state.

## Best Possible Solution

Review, live-validate, and land #74349 or an equivalent WhatsApp-plugin-owned replacement that makes QR scan advance to a connected state or surfaces a clear failure, while keeping WhatsApp-specific recovery inside the plugin and preserving the existing Baileys media-upload hotfix.
