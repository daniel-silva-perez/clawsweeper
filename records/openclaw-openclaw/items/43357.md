The branch expands Discord outbound mention rewriting to Unicode/CJK handles with email, URL, code-span, reserved-mention, formatted-mention, and account-scoping regressions, and also includes Yuanbao external channel catalog and contract-test updates.

## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment
