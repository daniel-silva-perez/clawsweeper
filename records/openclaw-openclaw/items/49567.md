## Summary

Keep open. Current main still lacks `tools.allow`/`tools.deny` on route or ACP bindings: the schema and exported binding types do not include a `tools` field, route resolution only carries the matched agent/session override, and the tool-policy pipeline has no binding-level policy step. Existing per-agent and channel/group policy surfaces are useful partial alternatives, but they do not implement the requested generic per-binding route/ACP restriction.

## Best Possible Solution

Add an additive core binding policy surface: extend route and ACP binding schemas/types with a `tools` policy using the existing allow/deny semantics, carry the matched binding policy through route/ACP resolution, apply it in the tool-policy pipeline after agent/group policy and before sandbox/subagent restrictions, and document/test validation plus enforcement for a group binding that denies `sessions_spawn` while DMs remain unaffected.
