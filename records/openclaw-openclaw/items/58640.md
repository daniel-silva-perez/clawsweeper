## What This Changes

The branch modifies `extensions/slack/src/monitor/context.ts` to warn when an explicitly denied Slack channel is dropped and consolidates the `runtime-env` imports.

## Best Possible Solution

Merge a tested warning-level denial log for the Slack explicit channel-disable path, then either adjust the linked issue scope before closure or leave a narrower follow-up for structured cross-channel denial logging with reason codes and rate limiting.

It is unclear as a complete fix for #58570 unless maintainers accept a Slack-only first step or keep the broader structured/deduped logging work tracked separately.
