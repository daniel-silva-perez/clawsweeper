## What This Changes

The issue reports incomplete terminal replies for Kimi K2.6 routes and timeout/degraded behavior from the gateway status probe on OpenClaw 2026.4.26.

Keep this issue open until maintainers review and either land #74289 or replace it with one canonical provider-level fix.
