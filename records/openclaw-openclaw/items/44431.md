## Summary

Keep open. Current main implements a useful subset of the field report, including selector targeting for many managed Playwright act actions, batched act execution, click delay handling, refs="aria" guidance, and tests for normalized batch dispatch. The issue still asks for several meaningful browser-tool surfaces that are not present: CDP inspectElement/event-listener inspection, snapshot captchaDetected signaling, refsStale or auto-refresh signaling, post-fill value verification, waitForRef semantics, and schema/docs alignment for the already-shipped selector/batch behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this open and split it into focused browser-plugin follow-ups. Treat selector targeting, managed-route batch actions, click delay, and refs="aria" guidance as partially shipped, then track the remaining pieces separately: inspectElement/CDP event-listener inspection, snapshot CAPTCHA detection, stale-ref signaling or auto-refresh behavior, post-fill verification or clearer slowly=true guidance, wait-by-ref semantics, and schema/docs alignment for supported act actions.
